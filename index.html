ABOUT US

About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation, founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization committed to creating lasting social impact across India.

With its base in Chhattisgarh, the foundation works through dedicated volunteers and professionals to support education, women empowerment, food distribution, animal welfare, environmental sustainability and youth skill development.

Our Credentials & Recognitions

  • ✔ Registered under Section 8
  • ✔ 80G & 12A Certified
  • ✔ CSR-1 Registered
  • ✔ NITI Aayog Registered
  • ✔ IAF ISO 9001:2015 Certified

Our Social Impact

  • ✔ 50,000+ meals distributed through Project SEVA
  • ✔ 20,000+ saplings planted under Project PRAKRITI
  • ✔ 30,000+ interns trained through Project VIKAS
  • ✔ 50+ stray animals fed daily through Project JEEV
  • ✔ Women empowerment through Project UDAAN
